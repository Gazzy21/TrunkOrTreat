TRUNK OR TREAT

Join us for a spooktacular evening at the Marlboro High School Trunk or Treat on October 29th! From 4 PM to 5:30 PM, bring your family and friends to enjoy a safe and festive Halloween experience. Decorated car trunks, delicious treats, and fun activities await! Don’t miss the chance to dress up, collect candy, and make lasting memories. We can't wait to see you there!
